Title: Improve mobile experience on results display

[Describe what the current experience is]
On mobile devices, the dashboard results display can be overwhelming with long text walls, some charts might break out of their containers or be hard to read, and the "back" navigation to upload another file feels hidden or clumsy.

[Describe what it should be]
The mobile dashboard should feature a streamlined, single-column layout with easily readable charts (perhaps stacked or swipeable), clear visual hierarchy breaking up long text with cards/accordions, and an always-accessible sticky header with navigation controls.

[Why this matters for users]
Many users will likely export and analyze their chats directly from their phones. A broken or cluttered mobile experience severely degrades trust and usability, making the premium feel fall flat.
