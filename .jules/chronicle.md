## 2024-05-20 — Initial Product/Growth Audit
**Discovery:** The product lacked clear shareability features (Share URL, Meta tags) which are critical for organic growth. "Pro features" were entirely missing from the UI, meaning we were losing out on signaling future value and capturing intent.
**Impact:** Adding the Share URL functionality allows users to send anonymous results to friends, driving viral acquisition. The placeholder Pro features start building a waitlist and validate demand for premium analytics.
**Action:** Next time, ensure every new feature considers its shareability potential from day one. Build social meta tags immediately for any public-facing product.
