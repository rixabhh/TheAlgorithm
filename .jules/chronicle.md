## 2026-10-15 — Shareability & Waitlist Capture
**Discovery:** Users love the Vibe Card but the current flow only lets them download it as a PNG, adding friction to sharing on Twitter or WhatsApp. Additionally, the Pro tier has no way to capture intent or build a mailing list.
**Impact:** Decreased virality due to sharing friction; lost monetization opportunities by not capturing high-intent users interested in Pro features.
**Action:** Implemented Web Share API for native mobile sharing of the Vibe Card, and added a client-side email capture form on the pricing page to gauge Pro feature demand.
