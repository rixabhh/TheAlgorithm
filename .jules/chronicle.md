## 2024-05-17 — Growth & Shareability Enhancements
**Discovery:** The product needed stronger sharing hooks (Canvas-based cards, URL links with base64 encoded anonymous stats) and SEO positioning (Open Graph and Twitter Meta tags) to lower the barrier for viral distribution. The lack of a clear monetization hook was also a missed opportunity.
**Impact:** Enhancing meta tags improves link preview rendering on social platforms, directly boosting click-through rates. The new Canvas-based share card provides an aesthetically pleasing, native "wrapped"-style image to post online without leaking raw chat data. Scaffolding a "Pro Features" section with a waitlist prompt starts qualifying high-intent users immediately.
**Action:** Future development should closely track engagement with the "Get Early Access" buttons to measure monetization potential. Continue refining the output of the Canvas share cards to maximize the aesthetic "wow" factor that drives screenshot sharing.
