## 2024-05-24 — Adding Viral Loops & Shareability

**Discovery:** The product generates interesting insights, but lacks a built-in viral loop. Users want to share their "relationship wrapped" with friends or partners, but previously had to manually screenshot the page which cuts off content and loses context.

**Impact:** Adding a single-click "Share Report" feature that generates an anonymized base64 URL enables effortless sharing. The addition of Open Graph and Twitter Card tags ensures these shared links (or just the main landing page) unfurl beautifully in iMessage, WhatsApp, and Twitter, dramatically improving click-through rates. Adding the pricing skeleton and locked Pro UI captures high-intent users immediately, acting as a waitlist for monetization.

**Action:** Prioritize features that allow users to generate social capital. Next time, focus on adding "Compare with Friends" functionality, as interpersonal comparison is a strong growth driver.
