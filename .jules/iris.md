## 2024-05-12 - Added Conversation Rhythm Analytics
**Discovery:** Expanding analytics to include structural conversation rhythms like peak activity hours and tracked apologies provides a much deeper insight into behavior patterns without parsing raw text in the LLM.
**Impact:** It gives the AI more data-driven behavioral signals to interpret relationship dynamics correctly (e.g., assessing accountability and scheduling alignment).
**Action:** When adding new metrics, always ensure they are included in the returned pipeline structure and exposed clearly in the dashboard HTML using native JS updates.
