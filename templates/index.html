Zero-Knowledge Privacy

Your conversations belong to you. Here's how we protect them.

1. Local PII Scrubbing

Before your file ever leaves your device, our JavaScript engine replaces all phone numbers and email addresses with standard placeholders right inside your browser.

2. No Databases. Pure RAM.

Our servers never write your chat file to a disk or a database. The text is parsed completely in active memory (RAM) and mathematically converted to statistics.

3. Instant Deletion

The millisecond the analysis completes, a Python `finally` block aggressively deletes all temporary session files. We keep zero logs of your conversations.

4. Safe LLM Usage

Your raw chat logs are NEVER sent to OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini. The LLM only receives an anonymous statistical payload (e.g. "Volume: 50, Sentiment: 0.8") to generate the coaching report.
