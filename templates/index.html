Zero-Knowledge Privacy

Your conversations belong to you. Here's how we protect them.

1. Local PII Scrubbing

Before your file leaves your device, our JavaScript engine replaces all phone numbers and emails with placeholders — right in your browser.

2. No Databases. Pure RAM.

Our servers never write your chat file to disk. The text is parsed completely in active memory (RAM) and converted to statistics.

3. Instant Deletion

The millisecond analysis completes, a Python `finally` block aggressively deletes all temporary session files. Zero logs of your conversations.

4. Safe LLM Usage

Your raw chat logs are NEVER sent to OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini. The AI only receives anonymous statistical payload to generate the coaching report.
